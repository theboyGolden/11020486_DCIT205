Undergraduate Courses

Explore our comprehensive list of undergraduate courses in computer science.

Computer Science Fundamentals

This foundational course covers the fundamental concepts of computer science, including algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving techniques.

  • Course Code: CS101
  • Credit Hours: 3
  • Prerequisites: None

Web Development Basics

Learn the essentials of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Build your first web pages and gain hands-on experience.

  • Course Code: CS205
  • Credit Hours: 4
  • Prerequisites: CS101

Database Design and Management

Explore the principles of database design and management. Understand SQL, normalization, and develop skills in creating and managing databases.

  • Course Code: CS310
  • Credit Hours: 3
  • Prerequisites: CS205
