ABOUT US

Welcome

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at The University of Ghana, Legon. Our department is committed to providing exceptional education and fostering innovative research in the field of computer science. In the era of Information Technology, Computer Science stands as the cornerstone. The profound study of computer science is imperative for society to fully harness the advantages offered by Information Technology and propel rapid economic advancements. This discipline encompasses the exploration and creation of applications essential for our daily interaction with computers, the intricate software systems supporting these applications, and the methodologies employed to produce applications and systems that exhibit reliability, correctness, and ethical behavior. At the core of Computer Science lies the art of computer programming. Employers seeking Computer Scientists expect adept programmers equipped with knowledge of contemporary techniques and tools, coupled with the ability to apply robust engineering principles to the development, construction, and maintenance of computer programs. The department offers a diverse range of courses, and students acquire skills that open doors to employment across various sectors of the economy. Our graduates are well-prepared for the computing industry, where they contribute to project teams or engage in research and development. The program aims to equip graduates with specialized skills, enabling them to make significant contributions to their teams. Furthermore, the curriculum serves as a foundation for future study or research, exposing students to established and emerging technologies, methods, and active research fields within Computer Science. The Department provides two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with options for Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1), as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge and skills in computer science, preparing them for successful careers and contributions to the ever-evolving world of technology.
